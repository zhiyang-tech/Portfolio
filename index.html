About Me

From a very young age, I have always been curious about how things work — from simple circuits to complex machines. This curiosity led me to pursue a Diploma in Electrical and Electronic Engineering specialising in Robotics at Singapore Polytechnic.

I am passionate about solving real-world problems through technology, combining both hardware and software to create smarter and more efficient systems. Beyond my studies, I actively participate in hackathons and robotics competitions.

I am driven by collaboration, curiosity and continuous learning, where I aim to turn innovative ideas into meaningful projects in Robotics and Full Stack Development.
